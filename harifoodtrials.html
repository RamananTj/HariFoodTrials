Kaali-Potato French Fries

Making video

Ingredients

  • Cauliflower
  • Potato
  • Cornflour
  • salt
  • chilli Powder
  • Salt Sizling

How to make?

  1. Boil adequate amount of Cauliflower and potatoes for 10 to 15minutes.
  2. Mash up well in the mixie.
  3. Add Cornflour and salt.
  4. Set up the mash over a plate and close it with a cover
  5. Freeze it for 2 hours.
  6. Cut the mash in rectangles for fingerchips.
  7. Oil fry it.
  8. add chilli powder & Salt sizling.

"that's it you made it on your own".
